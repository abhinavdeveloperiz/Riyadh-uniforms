About Riyadh Uniform

Riyadh Uniforms And Advertising is brand under Abyat Al Manara Contracting Establishment, which was founded in the year 2018 by Hairiyah Binth Mastour Bin Ali Bin Muhammad.

Riyadh Uniforms is focused on bringing World class Tailoring and advertising solutions in to the Kingdom Of Saudi Arabia. In the accordance with the Vision 2030, we are committed to provide all tailoring process to be done inside KSA. Our tailoring facility and showroom is located in Riyadh, KSA.

Our main Establishment Abyat Al Manara Contracting have multiple branches running in KSA across different sectors including Contracting, Advertisment, Clothing, Food & Bevarage etc.

In the dynamic and rapidly evolving landscape of Saudi Arabia, where tradition meets innovation, a professional image is paramount. At Riyadh Uniforms & Advertising, we understand that a uniform is more than just clothing; it is a powerful statement of brand identity, professionalism, and unity. We are a leading provider of high-quality, bespoke uniform solutions, dedicated to helping businesses across the Kingdom elevate their brand presence and empower their teams. Our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship, premium fabrics, and personalized design ensures that every uniform we create is a perfect blend of style, comfort, and durability, tailored to meet the unique needs of our diverse clients.
